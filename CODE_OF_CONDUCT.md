# Code of Conduct

Be direct. Be useful. Do not be a jerk.

## Expected

- Critique the work, not the person.
- Keep feedback specific.
- Assume public contributors do not know the private history of this project.
- Respect boundaries around private archives and personal data.

## Not Accepted

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks.
- Publishing private data.
- Derailing issues with noise after a maintainer has called time.
- Bigotry dressed up as bluntness.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments; close issues; block accounts; or decline contributions.

This is a small project, not a courtroom. Act accordingly.
